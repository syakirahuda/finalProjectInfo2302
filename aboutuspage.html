About Us Leviosa is an organization that sell vegetarian cuisines with various options of restaurants. In relation to Sustainable Goal Development, Goal 2: Zero Hunger, we listed all vegan food nearby the user for them to eat as their meal and delivered to the users specifically for someone who is starving but still too lazy to go to the store. We are also targeting Sustainable Development Goal 3: Good Health and Well-Being correspond to our tag line “Eat Right, Live Right” since vegetarian meals are also ideal for anyone who’s aiming for a healthy lifestyle and to lose weight healthily.
